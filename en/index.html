About Us

About TechTuit Taiwan

TECHTUIT TAIWAN CO., LTD. is the Taiwan office of TechTuit Group Japan, primarily responsible for product development, supplier development and procurement activities in Taiwan.

We support our group companies in identifying competitive Taiwanese suppliers, building long-term and stable partnerships, and assisting with product development, sample evaluation, component sourcing and supply chain communication.
